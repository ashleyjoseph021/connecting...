What is my Topic?

Computational Sustainabilty


Why computational sustainability?

I chose computational sustainability as my topic because it relates to my major. I am currenly a computer science major. When I was first thinking about what I wanted my topic to be I had a couple inital ideas, but none were really sitcking. I decided to see if there was any area of environmental stewarship that had to do with computer science and that's how I found out about computational sustainability. The more I read into computational sustanability the more I knew that this was what my topic should be. It invloved something I am passionate about so it was the perfect topic for me.
